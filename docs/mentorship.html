Overview

Every two months I will be offering a mentorship opportunity for two individuals who identify as a minority in the tech industry (women, BIPOC, Latinx, LGBTQ+ individuals, formerly incarcerated, etc). As a participant in this program, you will get one on one time with me that can be used in whatever ways best suit your needs.

Status

Open to applications until September 20, 2022. Mentorship cycle will run from October 1, 2022 to December 31, 2022. Apply here.

Program

What you can use this program for:
  • Advice and guidance on education
  • Advice and guidance on career
  • Resume review
  • Brainstorming support (technical or product)
  • Interview prep
  • Support in learning a new tool or technology
  • Reviewing and further exloring computer science and/or software engineering concepts
  • Troubleshooting, debugging, or code review (note: I will help point you in the right direction as opposed to providing a solution)
You may request to focus on other topics or ideas during our 1:1 time, however I reserve the right to decline for any reason.

Qualifications

In order to qualify for this opportunity, you must:
  • Identify as a minority in the tech industry.
  • Have at least a beginners foundation in computer science or software engineering
  • Be able to commit to meeting for 30 minutes every two weeks, and 45 minutes every month.

Expectations and further details

If you are chosen to be a part of this program, you are expected to commit to the following:
  • 1:1 meeting every 2 weeks (half hour)
  • group meeting every month (45 minutes)
You are also invited to join all of our Habibi Code events. Other information:
  • I will provide a Zoom link for all meetings
  • I am located in the Eastern Time Zone
  • I am unavailable Mondays through Fridays from 9am - 5pm ET
While I love full-stack development, I am not professionally experienced in front-end development. I have a Bachelor of Arts in Computer Science and Studio Art, and Masters of Science in Computer Science. My preferred programming language is Java, and I have experience in others and am able to adapt to most languages. I am currently working on a full-stack application for Prison Books Collective, a non-profit that sends free books to individuals incarcerated in North Carolina and Alabama; the app is being developed using Java, Spring Boot, Hibernate/JPA, MySQL, SvelteKit, HTML, Javascript, and CSS.

Interested?

Fill out this short application form, and I will be in touch within 3-5 business days.

If I feel that we will be a good fit for each other, I will schedule a short Zoom "get-to-know-you" session with you. Selected mentees will be notified by September 30, 2022.
